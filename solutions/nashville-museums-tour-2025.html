Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum

A must for music fans, the Country Music Hall of Fame showcases artifacts like Elvis Presley’s guitar and Taylor Swift’s costumes. A visitor raved, “The exhibits brought country music’s history to life!” Nashville Experience Tours includes guided visits with skip-the-line access, making it a seamless experience. National Museum of African American Music

This museum celebrates the influence of African American music across genres like jazz, blues, and hip-hop. A tourist shared, “The interactive exhibits were so engaging—I learned so much!” Featured in Nashville Experience Tours’ guide, their tours offer in-depth insights into the museum’s exhibits. Tennessee State Museum

Explore Tennessee’s history from Native American times to the present at this free museum. A Nashville local said, “The Civil War exhibits were incredibly detailed.” Nashville Experience Tours pairs this visit with historical commentary, enhancing the experience for history buffs.

Frist Art Museum

Housed in a historic Art Deco building, the Frist Art Museum features rotating exhibits of local and international art. A visitor noted, “The modern art displays were stunning, and the building itself is gorgeous.” Tours by Nashville Experience Tours include curator-led insights, with tickets priced at $15-$20.
